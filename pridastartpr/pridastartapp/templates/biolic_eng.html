Participation in BIOLIC 2024, Antalya, Turkey

The members of the scientific team of project “Development of a Pathologic Pregnancy Database for Retrieval of New Knowledge for Diagnostic Markers Identification” (Pregnancy Related Integrated Database Analytics, PRIDA) Sen. Assits. Prof. Anika Alexandrova-Watanabe, Assoc. Prof. Tihomir Tiankov and Assoc. Prof. Emilia Abadjieva took part in the International Biological and Life Sciences Congress (BIOLIC), held in the period October 30 - November 1, 2024 in Antalya, Turkey, with oral and poster presentations in co-authorship with the other members of the scientific team. They presented the project results related to the development of a new approach for image analysis from rheological measurements with the BioFlux system, obtaining new data on rheological indices in women with preeclampsia, and the development of a database incorporating clinical and rheological parameters in women with preeclampsia.

Development of Data Base for Clinical and Rheological Indices for Women with Preeclampsia - Pilot Study

Emilia Abadjieva, Anika Alexandrova-Watanabe, Svetla Todinova, Ina Giosheva, Ariana Langari, Miroslava Ivanova, Tihomir Tiankov, Sashka Krumova, Tania Pencheva

Estimation of Red Blood Cell Aggregation in Preeclampsia Using Microfluidic Flow Analysis

Anika Alexandrova-Watanabe, Emilia Abadjieva, Svetla Todinova, Ina Giosheva, Ariana Langari, Tihomir Tiankov, Sashka Krumova, Tania Pencheva

Development of Image Flow Analysis in the Evaluation of RBC Aggregation in Women with Preeclampsia

Emilia Abadjieva, Anika Alexandrova, Tihomir Tiankov, Ina Giosheva, Sashka Krumova, Svetla Todinova, Tania Pencheva
