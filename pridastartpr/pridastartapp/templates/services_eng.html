Work tasks

  • Selection of controls and patients and extraction of information on relevant clinical characteristics
  • Evaluation of the biophysical parameters of the blood in the studied pathologies
  • Development of an integrated database for patients with the studied pathologies
  • Analysis of possible correlations between the studied parameters through the application of various mathematical approaches
  • Development of а web-based application for accessing the database and analysing interrelationships between the individual factors included in it, in order to assess risk factors for the pathologies studied
  • Acquisition of unique scientific equipment
  • Dissemination activities informing the scientific community and the public about the achievements of the project

Experimental methods

  • Isolation of red blood cells and blood plasma from peripheral blood
  • Study of the rheological characteristics of red blood cells (degree of aggregation and deformability)
  • Study of the morphometric characteristics of red blood cells (shape, size, volume, roughness)
  • Study of the mechanical characteristics of erythrocytes (elastic modulus)
  • Assessment of the oxidative stress degree in blood plasma

Mathematical approaches for database analysis

  • Multivariate statistical analysis:
    • -classical correlation analysis
    • -principal component analysis
  • Intercriteria analysis:
    • -classical intercriteria analysis
    • -modifications of intercriteria analysis
