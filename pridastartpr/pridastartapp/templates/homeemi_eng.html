What is PRIDA ?


The aim of this project proposal is to create an integrated database for pathological pregnancy (Pregnancy Related Integrated Database Analytics - PRIDA). It combines data from clinical studies (including genetic, biochemical, immunological, hematological, hemorheological, etc.), registered in various medical centers in the country, and experimental biophysical data (morphological, mechanical and rheological characteristics of erythrocytes), obtained at the Institute of Biophysics and Biomedical Engineering at the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences, for healthy pregnant controls and patients with spontaneous abortion and preeclampsia.


The database will be accessible through a web-based application, providing opportunities for various regulated access rights. For the analysis of the interrelationships between the individual factors included in the database, possibilities for the application of classical statistical methods of analysis, as well as modern mathematical approaches developed at IBfhBME-BAS (intercriteria analysis), will be built-in.


The main goal of the project is to identify new diagnostic markers and prognostic factors for assessing the risk of adverse pregnancy outcomes at different stages.

On 29.05.2024, the Institute of Biophysics and Biomedical Engineering (IBfhBME) launched the implementation of the Project BG-RRP-2.011-0025 “Creating a database of pathological pregnancies and extracting new knowledge to identify diagnostic markers“ .


Integrated database for pathological pregnancy

The integrated database contains information about:

  • healthy pregnant controls;
  • patients with preeclampsia;
  • patients with spontaneous abortions.
