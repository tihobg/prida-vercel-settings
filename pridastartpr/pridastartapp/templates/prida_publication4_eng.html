Participation in Cell Physics 2025, Saarbrücken, Germany

Presentation of PRIDA project at CELL PHYSICS, 7-9.10.2025, Saarbrücken, Germany Sen. Assist. Prof. Ariana Langari, a member of the scientific team of the project "Development of a Pathologic Pregnancy Database for Retrieval of New Knowledge for Diagnostic Markers Identification" (Pregnancy Related Integrated Database Analytics, PRIDA), presented a poster at the scientific forum CELL PHYSICS 2025 International Meeting, held from 7 to 9 October 2025 in Saarbrücken, Germany. The poster was entitled "Nanomechanical and Rheological Characteristics of Red Blood Cells in Preeclampsia" and was co-authored by Velichka Strijkova, Miroslava Ivanova, Vesela Katrova, Anika Alexandrova - Watanabe, Emilia Abadjieva, Tihomir Tiankov, Ina Giosheva, Sashka Krumova, Tania Pencheva, and Svetla Todinova. The team's participation in the international forum attracted interest from the scientific community and highlighted the importance of research aimed at finding indicators for risk assessment in patients with preeclampsia in order to improve the diagnosis of complications during pregnancy
