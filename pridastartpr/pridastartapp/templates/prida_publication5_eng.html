Participation in the Scientific and Innovation Forum "International Forum on Advanced ICT Research and Innovation"

The “International Forum on Advanced ICT Research and Innovation” was held at Sofia Tech Park from September 29 to October 1, 2025. The forum was jointly organized by the Council for Science and Innovation in the Field of Information and Communication Technologies and the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences under investment C2.I2 "Increasing the Innovation Capacity of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences in the Field of Green and Digital Technologies", part of the National Recovery and Resilience Plan. At the invitation of the organizers, the project BG-RRP-2.011-0025 “Development of a Pathologic Pregnancy Database for Retrieval of New Knowledge for Diagnostic Markers Identification” (Pregnancy Related Integrated Database Analytics, PRIDA), funded by the European Union through the aforementioned mechanism, was also presented within the forum. The members of the scientific team of PRIDA project Sen. Assits. Prof. Anika Alexandrova-Watanabe and Assoc. Prof. Tihomir Tiankov presented the recent achievements of the project, including results related to the collection, selection, and systematization of retrospective and new data on clinical indicators and genetic mutations and their inclusion in an integrated database, along with experimentally studied biophysical and rheological parameters of blood cells in risk p regnancies. The design development of the PRIDA database and the creation of a web-based application for accessing the database were also presented, along with a demonstration of the capabilities for analysis of the relationships between individual factors, for risk assessment, etc.
